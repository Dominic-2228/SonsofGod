In this First part i want to Cover The idea of Son Ship.

In the Roman worldview, sonship did not primarily point backward to begetting, but forward to inheritance, often through the medium of adoption. In the time that Jesus was speaking, Jerusalem was under the Roman ruling and therefore, had a culture of Roman influence set on them. Jesus would speak of things that were natrual in the lives of those around Him, such as...

  • Seeds and the action of harvesting crops
  • The type of soil that one would build their house on
  • The cultural standard of women and the value of a certain coin

Each thing that ive just mentioned, Jesus would use to relate a point in the natural, that they could understand, and point to the kingdom of God and Himself.

I want to bring back out the quote i stated earlier, Sonship did not point to the beggetting or a boy, but an adoption into a family.
